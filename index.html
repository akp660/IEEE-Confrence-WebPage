The First “International Conference on Data, Computation and Communication" is an event focused on the fields of data, computation and communication.

(ICDCC-2024) 29 Nov 2024 & 30 Nov 2024

Scope of the Conference

  • Data science is an interdisciplinary field focused on extracting knowledge from typically large data sets and applying the knowledge and insights from that data to solve problems in a wide range of application domains. The conference focuses on following research area related to data science, machine learning and their application in computational physics, computational chemistry, computational biology and computational linguistics, computing paradigms like Cloud computing.
  • The integration of computational science and data science offers immense potential for advancing our understanding of chemical systems, accelerating drug discovery, optimizing materials design, and contributing to a wide range of scientific and industrial applications. The synergy between computational science and data science has the potential to revolutionize the way we understand and manipulate scientific systems.
