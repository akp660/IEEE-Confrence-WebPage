Registration Fee Policy for ICDCC-2024

Registration Fee: The registration fee for the International Conference on Digital Communication and Computing (ICDCC-2024) is non-refundable. Registered attendees are obligated to pay the full registration price, irrespective of whether they ultimately attend the conference or have already paid the registration charge.

Due to Unforeseen Circumstances: In the event that the conference is canceled due to unforeseen circumstances, such as natural calamities or any conditions or mandates imposed by the statutory or regulatory bodies of the Government of India, the entire amount paid as registration will be refunded without any clauses or conditions.

We understand that unexpected situations can arise, and we want to assure our participants that their financial investment in ICDCC-2024 will be fully reimbursed if the conference is canceled for reasons beyond our control. Your trust and commitment to our event are highly valued, and we are committed to providing a fair and transparent registration fee policy.

Should you have any questions or concerns regarding the registration fee or cancellation policy, please do not hesitate to contact our organizing committee for further assistance.
